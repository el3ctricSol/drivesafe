Commuting Tips

Creating a strong bike culture by getting more people to start commuting this way is important in fighting pollution.

Meeting other cyclists to commute with is a great way to stay entertained and compare biking experiences.

Remember to watch out for traffic and car doors.

Helmets prevent head injuries, which are the primary cause of death while commuting by bicycle.
