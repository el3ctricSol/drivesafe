The Environment

Bicycling is important to the health of Californians, including those who donâ€™t even have a bike. There is a significant environmental benefit to riding a bike once a week over the use of motorized vehicles.

Driving a car escalates emissions of harmful gasses, including carbon monoxide, methane, and nitrous oxide, but also leads to other types of pollution.

Water Pollution

Many people don't realize that motor vehicles are a major source of water pollution. Bikes do not leak the fluids our cars drip (oil, brake fluid, anti freeze), saving more clean water for us to drink.

Noise Pollution

High noise levels can contribute to cardiovascular effects and stress in humans. Bikes are quiet which helps create a quieter community.

Air Pollution

In one day, the use of bicycles over trucks and cars stops about seven tons of smog forming gases and a ton of inhalable particles from motorized vehicles from the air we breathe in California.
