Our Club

Our Goal is to educate you so you can educate your family and friends on the dangers of texting and driving.

Our Dream is to raise awareness and limit this dangerous behavior. There's one message we hope everyone receives loud and clear: the safest way to get from one place to another is to hang up and drive. Powering down your phone when you're behind the wheel can save lives, maybe even your own.

The fight to end distracted driving starts with you. Make commitment to drive phone-free.
