Tips

Tips#1: Out of sight out of mind; keep the phone out of arms reach.

Tips#2: Silence is king; turn off your phone when you’re driving.

Tips#3: Get an app; download anti-texting software on your phone.

Tips#4: Don’t text or call others when you know they are driving.

Tips#5: Have a texter while driving; if you have a friend or passenger in the car with you, have them answer the phone or respond to a text message.
