Some Facts About Domestic Violence

Within the United States 1 in 3 women are assaulted (slapped, pushed, shoved) by their partner within their lifetime.

1 in 4 women have experienced extreme physical violence (kicked, hit, slammed against an object) by their partner.

1 in 7 women have experienced some form of emotional abuse or psychological aggression (name calling, humiliation, manipulation) by their partner.

Domestic violence can kill. More than three women are murder by their partner each day.

Within a 24-hour period the National Domestic Hotline answered 760 calls in regards to domestic violence.

You are not to blame.

You are not alone.

There are others in this situation.

National Domestic Violence Hotline is your lifeline.

They can help you end the abuse.

Call the National Domestic Violence Hotline. 1-800-799-SAFE.
