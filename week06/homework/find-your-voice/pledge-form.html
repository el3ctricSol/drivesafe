End Domestic Violence in Your Life

This pledge is for your own sake. By taking this pledge you are willing to finding your voice. It is the first step to ending the violence in your life.

We encourage you to take the pledge. Information provided by you is safe and can be anonymous. Submitting a name is optional. By taking this pledge you will be helping us survey how many victims there are willing to stop the violence in their lives.

I will call the National Domestic Violence Hotline. 1-800-799-SAFE

I will no longer tolerate domestic violence in my life.

I will promote awareness and campaign against domestic violence.
