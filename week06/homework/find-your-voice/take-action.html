Are You a Victim of Domestic Violence?

Domestic Violence is pattern of abusive behavior (emotional, sexual or physical) that one uses to gain control and power over a person within the relationship.

If You Are a Victim of Domestic Violence

Call the National Domestic Violence Hotline. 1-800-799-SAFE.

Hotline advocates are available 24 hours 7 days a week. All calls are confidential and anonymous.

Hotline advocates can provide you with options for your situation and directly connect you to sources for immediate safety.
