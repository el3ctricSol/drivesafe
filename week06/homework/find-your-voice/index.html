Find Your Voice



By calling the National Domestic Violence Hotline

  • You are immediately connected with a hotline advocate.
  • An advocate will support and assist you during your crisis.
  • An advocate will help you develop a safety plan
  • An advocate will provide resources available in your area to help you in your situation.
  • An advocate will refer you to local agencies during your crisis.

All calls received by hotline are confidential and anonymous.

An advocate is available 24/7 to assist you.
