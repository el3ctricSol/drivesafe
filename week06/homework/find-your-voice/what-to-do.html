What You Can Do to Find Your Voice!

Domestic violence is not your fault. Youâ€™re not alone. Help is there for you. You no longer need to be afraid. Here are some suggestions that can help you get out of your situation.

Call 911 if you are in immediate danger.

Tell someone about your abuse.

Call the National Domestic Violence Hotline for help and advice. Hotline advocates will provide information and direct connect you to sources for immediate safety.

Create a safety plan.

A safety plan includes:

  • List of phone numbers (shelters,hotlines,friends/family.)
  • Escape route in your home
  • Emergency bag containing clothes, money,important documents,extra cell phone etc.
  • Review your safety plan often and implement safety plan when situation arises.

Seek medical attention if you have been physically abused.

No one deserves to be abused and it should not be tolerated.
