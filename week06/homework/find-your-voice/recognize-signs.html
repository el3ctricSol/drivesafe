Signs of Domestic Violence

  • Your significant other accuses you of cheating or being disloyal
  • Your significant other makes you feel unwanted and worthless
  • Your significant other physically hits, pushes, kicks, or chokes you
  • Your significant other verbally abuses you in private or in public
  • Your significant other isolates you from others

  • Your significant other constantly harasses you
  • Your significant other threatens to hurt you
  • Your significant other takes control of your money and keeps you captive in your house
