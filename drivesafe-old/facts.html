Facts

Fact#1: Five seconds is the average time your eyes are off the road while texting. When traveling at 55 mph, that's enough, time to cover the length of a football field.

Fact#2: 22% of teens who drive while distracted say it makes driving less boring.

Fact#3: 21% of teens who drive while distracted say they're used to being connected to people all the time.

Fact#4: 32% of teens who drive while distracted don't think anything bad will happen to them.

Fact#5: Though 95% of drivers surveyed said texting behind the wheel was unacceptable and unsafe, at least 21% admit to doing it anyway.
